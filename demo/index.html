Momentum ranking algorithm

A live simulation of a social feed ranked by momentum.

  • Each bubble is a post. Simulated users keep liking ♥ and commenting 💬 on them.
  • Height is the post's current rank (top is #1). Posts drift left as they age.
  • Color is instant momentum: posts heat up when attention arrives and cool down as it decays.
  • Size is the total attention a post has ever received.

Watch old, cold posts rocket back to the top when a burst of new likes hits them. That is what sets momentum ranking apart from simple decay.

Activity follows a day and night cycle with quieter weekends; the timeline at the bottom shows the pulse. Every post is born with a fixed level of interest to the community: interesting posts attract likes and comments, posts that interest nobody never receive any.

Click any post to make it go viral: it will receive a strong stream of likes and comments for a few hours and climb the ranking. Tweak every parameter of the algorithm in the panel on the right.
